2. What is the aiRealty Token (AIRT)?

The aiRealty Token (AIRT) is the cornerstone of our platform. It facilitates payments, provides access to premium AI tools, and enables investments in tokenized real estate. AIRT is tradeable on major cryptocurrency exchanges.

3. How does property tokenization work?

Property tokenization allows real estate assets to be divided into smaller, tradeable units represented by tokens. This approach makes real estate investment more accessible and allows individuals to own fractional shares of properties.
